SEC — 03 / PROCESS

How a survey engagement works

A straightforward, four-stage workflow from first contact to final deliverable.

01

Request & Scoping

Share your plot details and project goals. We confirm scope, timeline, and deliverables before mobilizing.

02

Field Data Capture

Licensed surveyors collect data on-site using GNSS, total stations, and drone equipment as required.

03

Processing & QA

Raw measurements are processed, cross-checked, and validated against survey-grade accuracy thresholds.

04

Delivery & Sign-off

You receive maps, reports, and CAD/GIS files, with a walkthrough of findings and next steps.
